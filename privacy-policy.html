Lumina: Echo of Light Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 29, 2026

Lumina: Echo of Light is an Android game designed to work without accounts, advertising, analytics, cloud saves, or online multiplayer.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. The game does not ask players to create an account and does not collect names, email addresses, precise location, contacts, payment information, health information, photos, messages, or other personal data.

Information Stored On Your Device

The game may save progress, settings, unlocked upgrades, achievements, stars, sparks, light cores, and best-run information locally on the player's device. This local save data is used only to continue the game between sessions and is not sent to us.

Sharing And Selling Data

We do not sell personal data and do not share personal data with third parties. Lumina: Echo of Light does not include third-party advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs.

Children And Families

Lumina: Echo of Light is intended for players aged 13 and older. The game is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Future Changes

If future versions add features such as online multiplayer, accounts, cloud saves, analytics, advertising, or purchases, this privacy policy and the Google Play Data safety form will be updated before those features are released.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support email listed on the Google Play store page for Lumina: Echo of Light.

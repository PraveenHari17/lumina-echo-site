# Lumina: Echo of Light

## Short Description

Explore shadowy ruins with echo pulses, dashes, puzzles, and daily challenges.

## Full Description

Lumina: Echo of Light is a glowing puzzle adventure where you guide a small light through ancient ruins, hidden paths, hazards, portals, enemies, and boss arenas.

Use Echo pulses to reveal the world around you, dash through danger, collect sparks and light cores, unlock upgrades, and return for daily challenges.

Play solo or use local duo mode on one device with mobile joystick controls. The game is built for quick sessions, with readable mobile controls, boss trials, upgrade progression, and offline play.

Features:
- Echo pulses that reveal hidden paths and hazards
- Dash movement for quick escapes and advanced routes
- Puzzle rooms, portals, enemies, and boss encounters
- Solo play and local duo mode on one device
- Daily rewards, sparks, stars, light cores, and upgrades
- Offline play with local progress saved on your device

Lumina does not require an account, does not show ads, and does not collect personal data.
